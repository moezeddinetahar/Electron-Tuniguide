Tunisian National Railway Company (SNCFT)

SNCFT is the national railway company in Tunisia, responsible for operating and managing the country's railway network.

  • Operates passenger and freight train services across various regions of Tunisia.
  • Plays a crucial role in transporting people and goods across the country.
  • Contributes to connectivity and mobility of citizens and goods in Tunisia.

SNCFT is dedicated to providing safe, efficient, and reliable railway services to meet the transportation needs of the public and industry.
